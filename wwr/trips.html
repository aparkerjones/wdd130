Snake River Adventure

Experience the thrill of Class III-IV rapids on the legendary Snake River. This full-day adventure includes lunch, professional guides, and breathtaking canyon scenery. Perfect for adrenaline seekers and nature lovers!

Sunset Rapids Run

Paddle into the golden hour with our evening Sunset Rapids Run. Includes snacks, a riverside campfire, and unforgettable views as the sun sets over the water. Ideal for couples and groups.

Family Float Trip

Enjoy a gentle, scenic float suitable for all ages. Our Family Float Trip features calm waters, wildlife viewing, and plenty of splashy fun. Great for kids, grandparents, and first-time rafters.
